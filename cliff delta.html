What is Cliff's Delta?

Cliff's Delta is a non-parametric measure of effect size. It indicates how often values in one group are larger than those in another group. A positive value suggests the isolated group tends to have higher values, while a negative value suggests the control group has higher values.

Interpretation:

  • 0 to ±0.147: Small effect
  • ±0.148 to ±0.33: Medium effect
  • ±0.33 and above: Large effect
